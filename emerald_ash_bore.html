What Are Emerald Ash Borers?

Emerald Ash Borers are exotic beetles. The adults nibble on ash tree foliage, this causes little damage. The larvae feeds on the inner bark of ash trees. This plays havoc on the tree's ability to transport nutrients and water. Since the discover, this insect has killed millions of ash trees.


What Does This Mean For Jedds Tree Care?

Ash trees make up a large portion of the trees in the US. The best way to prevent the spread this beetle is keeping ash trees healthy, far away from other infected trees, and using insecticidal sparys regularly.


How To Identify Whether Or Not A Tree Has Been Affected

These beetles leave D-shaped holes in the wood. If the bark is removed, you may even see the winding, serpentine tunnels.
